Now, you can generate a full-fledged app with just a prompt!

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/pEAtLQ4ESAU?rel=0" width="100%" height="500px" title="YouTube video"></iframe>

## A step-by-step guide to generating your first app

**Step 1:** Head to [Softr dashboard](https://studio.softr.io/dashboard) and select Generate with AI

**Step 2**: Select what type of app you want to generate:

* Client Portal

* Internal tool

* Directory

* Membership platform

* Website
<Frame caption="Softr AI App Generator">![Softr AI App Generator](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2Fbfbeb81e-4868-49e1-af2d-e40d162f88b4%2FScreenshot_2023-10-04_at_18.41.02.png&blockId=bf758109-7895-48e1-aae6-020b819e41bf&projectId=1032)</Frame>

**Step 3:** Describe your app idea with a short sentence. Or get inspired by some of our suggestions:

* A client portal for a VC fund to share updates with their investors.

* An employee intranet for knowledge sharing and communicating company updates.

* A directory of companies specializing in VR content and VR experiences.

* A membership platform where businesses keen on franchising can access insights, receive legal advice, and learn from success stories.

* A website for a blockchain consultancy company, presenting successful blockchain integrations, educational workshops, and client success stories.

**Step 4:**  Preview the app to select your preferred theme and accent color.
<Frame caption="Select a theme and accent color">![Select a theme and accent color](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2Ffaf35b0c-9d78-47d5-8010-b5945e269bfc%2FScreenshot_2023-10-05_at_11.20.54.png&blockId=0f4d6b2d-cc6d-4901-ba42-8d2b6b249174&projectId=1032)</Frame>

**Step 5**: **Generate your app!** ✨  *Note: Bigger applications might take a few seconds.*

**Step 6:** You will see **an interactive preview** once an app is generated. Feel free to explore it like it’s a real app! Sign in as different users and check out different pages and functionality.
<Frame>![Notion image](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2F48e6f29a-23f3-412b-8f35-cceed8679cc6%2Feea822201a936e7fad39d70f250a67bc.gif&blockId=d9cfeb99-d911-4dd8-800e-970a7e4a1b3f&projectId=1032)</Frame>

**Step 7**: If you like the result, **share** it with others or [go to the studio](https://studio.softr.io/dashboard) to customize it further.

## What to expect from an AI-generated app?

Depending on the app type, your generated app can include the following:

* Generated data in [Google Sheets](/data-sources/1YYStCJukT15Lh2j93qo9A/google-sheets/jznC9Jr3Jri224AVmHVn4a) (you can connect your own data in Google Sheets or [Airtable](/data-sources/1YYStCJukT15Lh2j93qo9A/airtable/jHX7kfH7QvkVcELQvS3VoX))

* Multiple [Pages and Blocks](/core-concepts/c5VwEbsWtTfPyUZjmEKvfx/pages-and-blocks/jFkbKmHNMLgvriQvosQapg)

* Sample [Users](/add-and-manage-users/pxnkp9H3qyEVMF6XUceqnj/users-tab/5JTzjtbvp8Skemd26A9ho9) and [User Groups](/add-and-manage-users/pxnkp9H3qyEVMF6XUceqnj/overview/6XQfKMeKHUvBcAjKvLs3g3)

* [Action buttons](/actions/ntEfT9zSJk6aY54G6W5KGU/action-buttons-overview/idg8Fqf2kjFwcx6kyP3jNq)

* [Sign-in](/add-and-manage-users/pxnkp9H3qyEVMF6XUceqnj/signin-form/gYwm7wWsqb2bRyJFUreSzN) page

* [User Profiles](/add-and-manage-users/pxnkp9H3qyEVMF6XUceqnj/user-profile/jVikpwBm67W9wozwyDXtjs)

# How to Customize an AI-generated App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your data">
    AI-generated apps already have generated data in Google Sheets connected to the app. You need to copy the database to your Google account to make it your own and customize it as you wish.

    <Info>

    If you want to connect your existing data, you can do that once the template data is copied.
    </Info>
    <Frame caption="Copy generated data">![Copy generated data](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2Fab7db574-adbc-40e3-a3ea-cd881370bfff%2FScreenshot_2023-10-05_at_17.19.12.png&blockId=1359a723-d691-8055-b8e0-cc883e02c4c1&projectId=1032)</Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your app">
    The Studio is your workspace where you design and build your app. In the **left toolbar,** you can access [Pages](/core-concepts/c5VwEbsWtTfPyUZjmEKvfx/pages-and-blocks/jFkbKmHNMLgvriQvosQapg). Create new ones or edit existing pages to your liking.
    <Frame>![Notion image](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2F7a45bde1-5df0-4f53-bdbf-b3cf3fa653ef%2FScreenshot_2023-10-05_at_17.51.55.png&blockId=1359a723-d691-80e0-baff-c31d9b97f710&projectId=1032)</Frame>

    Each page contains [Blocks](/core-concepts/c5VwEbsWtTfPyUZjmEKvfx/pages-and-blocks/jFkbKmHNMLgvriQvosQapg); you can customize the style and function of existing ones or add new blocks.
    <Frame>![Notion image](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2Fa6fe0d6b-e993-45e8-81c4-575bb3c049e8%2F2c5030521373273e30a4d42b7f439026.gif&blockId=1359a723-d691-809a-9bff-dd03999b6b3e&projectId=1032)</Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Users and User groups">
    Generated apps can come with generated user roles. You can update them in the [Users](/add-and-manage-users/pxnkp9H3qyEVMF6XUceqnj/users-tab/5JTzjtbvp8Skemd26A9ho9) setting and customize [User Groups & Permissions](/add-and-manage-users/pxnkp9H3qyEVMF6XUceqnj/users-tab/5JTzjtbvp8Skemd26A9ho9)
    <Frame>![Notion image](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2F20fad166-b53f-459c-9fd6-e678b8109695%2FScreenshot_2023-10-05_at_17.56.44.png&blockId=1359a723-d691-802c-be08-f5675f0fe0ab&projectId=1032)</Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review app settings">
    Before publishing your app, review the app [Settings](/application-settings/n3K2UEfpC9BpHgakMzUU5Y/general-settings/7YdGy1J8X2tjJSv8BGvWQU) to define custom domains, integrations, SEO, and others
    <Frame>![Notion image](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2F103545f4-0edb-400b-9e7f-12993ef9f6b4%2FScreenshot_2023-10-05_at_18.06.34.png&blockId=1359a723-d691-8053-b0a1-dc57be1adb46&projectId=1032)</Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview & Publish">
    In the upper right corner, you have the **Preview** button, which can be used to preview your app at any moment, and the Publish button when it’s time to go live! Click the **Publish** button on the top right corner. After publishing, you can always make changes in your app and publish your changes the same way.
  </Step>
</Steps>
