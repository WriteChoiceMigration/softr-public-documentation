AtomChat is a great solution for adding a group chat or video conferencing functionality for your users if you're building an online community, a social network, an internal collaboration tool, and so on.

[Here](https://help.atomchat.com/support/solutions/articles/82000892680-add-atomchat-to-your-softr) you can find a detailed step-by-step guide on integrating AtomChat with Softr. You can also [add a chat option directly to user profiles](https://help.atomchat.com/support/solutions/articles/82000892793-chat-with-a-user-from-their-profile-softr).
