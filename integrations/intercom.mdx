Intercom is a messaging platform that allows you to communicate with prospective and existing customers. Here, we'll go through the process of adding the Intercom chat widget to your Softr app.

## Locating Intercom Workspace ID

First, you need to locate your Intercom **workspace ID** (app ID). It can be found in the URL of any page you have open in Intercom. It's the code that comes after **apps/**.
<Frame caption="Locating the workspace ID">![Locating the workspace ID](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2Fad4b941c-39da-46aa-9c19-b04040fcf961%2Flocating-workspace-id.png&blockId=435a836f-8985-42a3-bb5c-e24a8237074d&projectId=1032)</Frame>

The workspace ID can also be found in Settings. In your Intercom account, hover over the account icon in the lower left corner and go to **Settings**.
<Frame caption="Navigating to Settings">![Navigating to Settings](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2F0ce97149-3c56-4c54-930b-80a9d298915f%2Fsettings.png&blockId=6c7e75e2-be9c-40e9-be1b-5aaa6df66bb7&projectId=1032)</Frame>

Then go to **Installation => Web** and choose the **With code** option under **Install chat for visitors.**
<Frame caption="Widget installation">![Widget installation](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2F5d86cf85-fb98-4078-8b4a-5c58d7f1bf46%2Fwidget-installation.png&blockId=d16285ba-635f-4c5f-bc6e-3e9c3fe9f2a0&projectId=1032)</Frame>

Scroll down and copy the **app_id**.
<Frame caption="Copying app ID">![Copying app ID](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2F430eb1eb-e901-44e8-b993-538de33d57e0%2Fapp-id.png&blockId=0c664470-e9ff-462b-acfb-46e1c2ecdce5&projectId=1032)</Frame>

## Adding Workspace ID in Softr Studio

So, after copying the workspace ID, navigate to **Settings => Integrations => Intercom** in your Softr Studio and paste the ID.
<Frame caption="Adding Intercom ID in Softr">![Adding Intercom ID in Softr](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2F73ce37e7-5873-4793-8e19-5dc0772bf779%2Fadding-intercom0id-in-softr.png&blockId=95f5d64a-9d08-4d47-a77c-95f56abd9b35&projectId=1032)</Frame>

That's it. As soon as you save your settings and republish your Softr app, the Intercom chat widget will start appearing.
<Frame caption="Intercom widget added to site">![Intercom widget added to site](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2F709af14d-81a5-453d-b8da-1b2945e77380%2Fintercom-widget-added-to-site.png&blockId=c6cc2c6c-2f60-41e3-b492-26e981c8987f&projectId=1032)</Frame>
