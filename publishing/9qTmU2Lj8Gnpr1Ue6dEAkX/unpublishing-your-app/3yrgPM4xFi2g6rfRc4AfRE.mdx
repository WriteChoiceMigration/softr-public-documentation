You can easily unpublish your Softr app by clicking on the Settings tab in Softr Studio, then clicking on General.
<Frame>![Notion image](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2Fb76f5c1e-b35e-4f91-a3b9-26ec82d6df1a%2FScreenshot_2024-11-27_at_1.31.33_PM.png&blockId=14b9a723-d691-80f9-9167-c320def5c14a&projectId=1032)</Frame>

Scroll to the bottom of the General settings options and click on Unpublish Application:
<Frame>![Notion image](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2F477fa9a8-d066-47d0-9928-e69be8ab0154%2FScreenshot_2024-11-27_at_1.33.08_PM.png&blockId=14b9a723-d691-8051-9ff8-d1048da7b9b3&projectId=1032)</Frame>

Click on Unpublish to confirm and unpublish the application. Note that your application will no longer be available on custom domains or Softr subdomains until you re-publish, but will always be available for Preview from within Softr Studio:
<Frame>![Notion image](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F484adde5-19ba-4c92-b797-4cbdd4ad462b%2F59381f38-7dac-4ce2-9fbb-d0a85fc2785d%2FScreenshot_2024-11-27_at_1.33.46_PM.png&blockId=14b9a723-d691-8055-9bcc-ee4e6d885848&projectId=1032)</Frame>
