This is a simple block with some text and one or more CTA buttons that accomplish certain actions. The block has a **Title**, **Subtitle**, and a **Button(s)**.

# Common Block Features

## Title, Subtitle, and Background Image

These settings are located under the **Features** tab of block settings, and here you customize the **Title** and **Subtitle** texts and can add a **Background Image** for the whole block.
<Frame caption="Title, Subtitle, and Background Image">![Title, Subtitle, and Background Image](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2Fb3886b2e-cd93-4de1-8508-90b1730149e3%2Ftitle-subtitle.png&blockId=1a0c5fec-db3a-48aa-974b-44d13a47f25d&projectId=1032)</Frame>

## Button

By default, there's only one button, but you can use the **Add Button** option to add more buttons.
<Frame caption="Adding a button">![Adding a button](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2F849b5dd8-145a-45a0-9b61-57d0486187c9%2Fadd-button.png&blockId=94a07edd-f162-417c-8fbc-3bbe1bf371e8&projectId=1032)</Frame>

In the [Call to action with email capture](/6772b65a34c84bfdbe1bc16e8b03f525) layout the button allows to capture the user's email, but in the rest of the layouts it has the following **Action** options:

* **Open page** to open another page within the app;

* **Scroll to section** to scroll to a specific section within the page;

* **Open external url;**

* **Open Modal** to open another page within a pop-up.
<Frame caption="Button actions">![Button actions](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2F873b4528-7209-41dd-9344-57bf4595af7e%2Factions.png&blockId=92a7c1e4-5c75-406f-b3ed-4f66602fa8fd&projectId=1032)</Frame>

## Layout: Call to action with email capture
<Frame caption="Call to action with email capture">![Call to action with email capture](https://media.helpkit.co/proxy?url=https%3A%2F%2Fs3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2Fsecure.notion-static.com%2F7713765b-e321-47b3-b5ed-c4660885f298%2Fcta-with-email_capture.png&blockId=13f9a723-d691-80db-a052-c1b4ab87ecda&projectId=1032)</Frame>

In this layout, instead of a simple CTA button, you have an email capture area. You can set the button **Action** to send the captured email to one of the supported services (more details in the [Integrations](/integrations/etS18kJqm1fuD58KCsjC3z/mailchimp/2GvSLRGrgMfe8S3drMWao1) section) or simply forward it to the specified email address.
